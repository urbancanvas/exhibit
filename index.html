Brand Story

The streets are the testaments to the arts of urban life: different places tell different tales. From sidewalk graffiti, murals on the wall, slaps stuck to a signboard– every art on the street speaks a story. Urban Canvas takes you on a walk-trip in the streets: the alley, the block, and the ville where artists showcase their imagination on how they see the wonders of urban life.
