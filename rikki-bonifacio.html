About me

Rikki Lou Bonifacio is a visionary artist who seamlessly blends the raw energy of street photography with the captivating allure of digital poster art. With an eye for detail and a deep understanding of contemporary aesthetics, Rikki has mastered the art of capturing the essence of urban life through his lens, while also transforming those moments into visually appealing digital compositions. Drawing inspiration from a wide array of sources, such as street art, pop culture, and urban subcultures, Rikki's digital posters manifest a vibrant fusion of realism and imagination.
